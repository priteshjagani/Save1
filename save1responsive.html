How Do I Intervene? (Right)

1. Trust your gut- if a situation seems bad, it probably is.
2. Ask for help- ask an RA, friend, or the bartender to go with you when intervening
3. Cause a distraction- ask the person in danger to go to the bathroom with you, or invite them to sit with you and your friends
4. Be aware of your surroundings
5. Do not put yourself at risk

Consent 101 (Right)

1. Giving and asking for consent shows respect for your partner and yourself
2. It is NOT consent if there is any kind of force or coercion
3. Consent is voluntary and mutual
4. Consent can be withdrawn at any time
5. A person who is intoxicated or unconscious cannot legally give consent
